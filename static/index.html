Exploring Data Within the Film Industry

The purpose of this project is to display the international film market and expose viewers to which countries are producing film around the world.

After assembling the dataset, we used SQLite to set up a database to serve our data to our Visualizations. You will find three visulalizations within this site via the dropdown menu: Map, Directors Table, and Graphs
